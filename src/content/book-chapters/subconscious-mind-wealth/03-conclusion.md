The most extraordinary aspect of harnessing the power of the subconscious mind is that, once mastered, the very notion of effort dissipates. By connecting to the subconscious, we can overcome lethargy, lack of motivation, or depleted energy. It’s important to remember that humans are always engaged in some activity, be it sleeping, watching television, or playing video games. Our bodies possess the inherent ability to ‘do something’ throughout our lives; however, there are times when our desires and needs conflict. We may consciously aspire to learn new financial techniques, yet our body craves the escapism of Assassin’s Creed IV® or the latest TV show. Recognize that whenever you succumb to perceived laziness or procrastination, it's the subconscious mind choosing a path contrary to the conscious mind. By reprogramming the subconscious to align with the conscious mind's motivations, the entire concept of motivation becomes obsolete, and we effortlessly pursue what our rational minds deem essential (including recreation).

It is crucial to understand that our connection to infinite abundance, however you perceive this limitless force or entity, follows this pathway: Conscious -  - > Subconscious -  - > Infinite. Many of the actions I’ve discussed to strengthen this connection evoke a sense of spiritual fulfillment and purpose in life. This bond links you to the divine, the superconscious, the cosmos, or any concept of your choosing. The pathway serves as a conduit to infinite power and wealth, enabling you to create and attract prosperity. The speed at which you accomplish your goals directly corresponds to your ability to forge this bridge to the subconscious. To amass wealth, you must deepen your connection to the subconscious. I am not offering you business advice or specific investment strategies. Instead, the answer to “How can I achieve great wealth?” lies in mastering your own mind - hacking into your subconscious. Every answer to your wealth - related questions and beyond resides within you. Concentrate on fortifying this Conscious -  - > Subconscious -  - > Infinite pathway.

As you strengthen the Conscious -  - > Subconscious -  - > Infinite pathway, the reverse pathway also intensifies. Indeed, the reverse pathway. As you reinforce the Conscious -  - > Subconscious -  - > Infinite connection, you will attune yourself to the very voice of the divine, the cosmos, or the superconscious. You will receive financial guidance from Infinite -  - > Subconscious -  - > Conscious. It is at this moment that you confront the reality that may have once defeated you, and suddenly you can perceive the very fabric of the hologram, fearing nothing - not even death - and knowing that you possess the power to reshape the world to reflect your boundless potential. Enjoy the journey!
