This chapter teaches the reader how not to collapse into the failure-world.

## Key Sections
- Failure as feedback.
- Pain as signal, not sentence.
- The danger of global conclusions: "I failed, therefore I am a failure."
- Reframing without denial.
- How to extract usable information from disappointment.
- The recovery loop: pause, interpret, learn, act.

## Practice: The Failure Translation Sheet
The reader translates:

What happened?

What did I make it mean?

What else could it mean?

What did it reveal?

What model should I update?

What is the next action?
