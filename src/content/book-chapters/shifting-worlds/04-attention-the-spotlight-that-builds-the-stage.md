## Chapter Promise
This chapter explains attention as the first tool of world selection.

What we attend to grows in psychological relevance. The mind begins to collect evidence around it. Opportunity, threat, beauty, insult, possibility, failure-each can become the center of a world.

## Key Sections
- Attention as selection.
- Why the same environment produces different realities for different people.
- The Reticular Activating System as a practical metaphor for noticing what matters.
- Attention and emotional energy.
- The discipline of choosing what gets mental oxygen.

## Practice: The Seven-Day Attention Experiment
For one week, the reader tracks what they repeatedly notice. Then they intentionally selects one new signal to notice daily: opportunity, beauty, progress, discipline, kindness, courage, or useful feedback.
