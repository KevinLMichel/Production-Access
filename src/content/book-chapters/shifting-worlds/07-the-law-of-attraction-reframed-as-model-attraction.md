## Chapter Promise
This chapter presents the law of attraction through the lens of mental models.

The core point: dominant thoughts and beliefs shape perception; perception shapes action; action shapes probability; probability shapes outcome. The "attraction" is not reduced to crude wishing. It becomes the disciplined alignment of attention, emotion, speech, belief, and behavior toward an intended world.

## Key Sections
- Why intention matters.
- Why belief without action decays.
- Why action without belief loses force.
- Why visualization works as rehearsal, orientation, and identity training.
- The deeper possibility: that consciousness participates in reality more mysteriously than materialist models currently explain.
