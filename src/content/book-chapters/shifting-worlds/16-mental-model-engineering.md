## Chapter Promise
This chapter gives the reader a practical system.

## Key Sections
- Name the model.
- Test the model.
- Keep what works.
- Discard what weakens.
- Build a better model.
- Install it through repetition.
- Validate it through results.

## Practice: The Model Lab
For any belief, the reader asks:

What does this model predict?

What emotions does it create?

What actions does it encourage?

What outcomes does it tend to produce?

Is it accurate?

Is it useful?

What model would be more truthful and powerful?
