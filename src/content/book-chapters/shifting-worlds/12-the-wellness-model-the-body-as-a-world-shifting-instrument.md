## Chapter Promise
Wellness is not treated as a side issue. It is the biological foundation of world-shifting.

## Key Sections
- The mind does not float above the body; it speaks through the body.
- Sleep, food, movement, breath, and posture as model-supporting forces.
- Why exhaustion creates darker worlds.
- Why vitality expands perceived possibility.
- The body as the instrument through which intention becomes action.

## Practice: The Body-World Audit
The reader scores sleep, nutrition, movement, breath, posture, and recovery. Then they choose one bodily upgrade that would most improve their mental world.
