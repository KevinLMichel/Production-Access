## Chapter Promise
Visualization is presented as rehearsal, emotional alignment, probability training, and communication with the subconscious.

## Key Sections
- Why the mind rehearses futures.
- The difference between fantasy visualization and embodied visualization.
- Seeing the result versus seeing the process.
- How athletes, performers, and leaders mentally pre-live desired outcomes.
- Many-world visualization: exploring multiple possible outcomes, then choosing the empowering path.

## Practice: Many-Worlds Visualization
The reader imagines three futures:

The world of avoidance.

The world of partial effort.

The world of full alignment.

Then they step into the aligned world and notice posture, breath, words, beliefs, choices, and next actions.
