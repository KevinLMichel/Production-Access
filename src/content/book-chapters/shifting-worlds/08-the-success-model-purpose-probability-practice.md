## Chapter Promise
Success is framed as a navigational model.

The chapter argues that success is not merely desire plus effort. It is desire clarified into purpose, purpose translated into models, models tested through practice, and practice refined through feedback.

## Key Sections
- Purpose as the guiding vector.
- Probability as the field of possible outcomes.
- Practice as world-selection through repetition.
- The danger of vague dreams.
- How clear goals alter perception and behavior.

## Practice: The Success Equation
Purpose × Model × Practice × Feedback = Shifted World

The reader fills in each part for one goal.
