## Chapter Promise
A practical daily routine.

Morning:

Set the world: "What world am I choosing today?"

Name the identity: "Who am I being today?"

Visualize one key moment.

Speak one aligning sentence.

Choose three world-building actions.

Midday:

Check the model.

Adjust attention.

Reset the body.

Evening:

Review evidence.

Rewrite one limiting interpretation.

Record one sign of progress.

Prepare the next shift.
