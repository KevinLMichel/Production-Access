This bibliography gathers works consulted or recommended for readers who want to study the historical, psychological, and cultural ground beneath this book. It is not a note-by-note apparatus. The manuscript uses these fields as background: slavery and the African diaspora, African civilizations, family history, Malcolm X, James Baldwin, Maya Angelou, intergenerational trauma, media systems, attention, identity formation, and disciplined self-regulation.
