Public comment is not a performance. It is a record.

## Template

| Line | Draft |
| --- | --- |
| My name is [name], and I live/work in [place]. | &nbsp; |
| I am speaking about [specific agenda item or issue]. | &nbsp; |
| The problem is [one or two sentences]. | &nbsp; |
| The evidence is [bill, date, incident, budget line, policy, public record, or direct experience]. | &nbsp; |
| The requested action is [specific vote, delay, amendment, audit, meeting, record release, or policy change]. | &nbsp; |
| Please state publicly who is responsible for the next step and when residents will receive an answer. | &nbsp; |
| Follow-up contact or record request | &nbsp; |

## Rule

Be specific enough that silence becomes visible.
