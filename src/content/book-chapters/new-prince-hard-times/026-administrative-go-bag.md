The gate is often boring. Prepare anyway.

## Keep copies

- IDs and proof of address.
- Income records and tax records.
- Lease, utility bills, insurance cards.
- Benefit letters and case numbers.
- School, clinic, veteran, union, or legal-aid contacts.
- Denial letters, appeal forms, screenshots, and deadlines.
