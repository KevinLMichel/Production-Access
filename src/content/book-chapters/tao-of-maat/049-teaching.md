Knowledge is a candle – lighting another’s wick does not dim your own flame.<br />
Share what you know with those who seek wisdom.<br />
A teacher once helped you take your first steps; honor them by guiding another.<br />
Be patient with the student who struggles, for even the mighty oak begins as a tender shoot.<br />
In teaching, you learn; in giving, you receive.<br />
Hide not your wisdom out of jealousy or pride – that is hoarding grain while others starve.<br />
Let your teachings be true and kind, a beacon not a burden.<br />
Thus you keep the flame of Ma’at burning bright through the generations.
