I said to my soul in sorrow:<br />
“Better to end this life than to endure such pain.<br />
My heart is weary; my name feels lost,<br />
my lot is endless suffering with no friend to hear my cry –<br />
like a bird trapped in a sunless twilight.”<br />
And my soul answered:<br />
“Pain is a night that passes.<br />
Death will come in its time; do not hasten it.<br />
A day of clarity will follow this day of clouds.<br />
Live now, do good now, and trust the dawn to come.<br />
When your hour arrives, we will go together –<br />
your heart at peace, your deeds shining behind you.”
