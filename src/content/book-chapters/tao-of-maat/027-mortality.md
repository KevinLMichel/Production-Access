All who walk under the sun will one day walk into the west.<br />
Death is as natural as sunset after day.<br />
Do not fear it, but live so that when it comes, your heart is light.<br />
No riches accompany you beyond the tomb; only the tale of your deeds echoes in eternity.<br />
Let that tale be one of kindness and courage.<br />
One who is mindful of death lives more fully – each day becomes a precious gift, not a right.
