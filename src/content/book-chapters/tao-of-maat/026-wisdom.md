A fool thinks he is perfect; a wise person knows they are not.<br />
The fool takes pride in loud opinions; the wise asks questions in quiet.<br />
Wisdom is more listening than speaking.<br />
It grows not from age alone, but from an open heart and mind.<br />
The river of knowledge flows into the sea of wisdom only when the channels of pride are cleared.<br />
Do not be wise in your own eyes.<br />
Seek counsel and ponder deeply – wisdom will enter your soul like the dawn.
