When problems weigh you down, step back and look to the stars.<br />
You will see your troubles shrink as your vision expands.<br />
The stars have seen countless generations come and go; under their light, king and peasant are the same.<br />
Remember how small you are in the vastness of creation – not to belittle your worth, but to right-size your worry.<br />
The river still flows and the sun still rises, regardless of our daily triumphs or sorrows.<br />
Find comfort in this steady rhythm.<br />
Align yourself with the grand scheme of things, and your heart will find peace in its rightful place.
