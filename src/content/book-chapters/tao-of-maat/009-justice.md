Do not tilt the scales against the helpless.<br />
The orphan, the widow, the stranger – Ma’at hears their silent plea.<br />
If you take from the poor to fatten yourself, you feed on poison.<br />
Wealth gained by injustice is a pyramid built on sand: it will collapse and bury you.<br />
True justice shines like the sun on all, without favor.<br />
The breath of the divine lives in the cry of the wronged – take care lest it blow against you.
