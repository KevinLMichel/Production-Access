Whatever you do, do it with care and excellence.<br />
A shoddy offering dishonors the maker more than the gods.<br />
Your work reflects your spirit.<br />
If you are a scribe, let your lines be true; if a builder, let each stone be firm.<br />
Quality matters more than show.<br />
Labor patiently, and let no detail be too small for your attention.<br />
Through devoted craft, you partake in creation’s perfection.<br />
Even humble tasks gain dignity when done well.<br />
By striving for excellence in your deeds, you bring Ma’at into the works of your hands and leave a legacy your soul can be proud of.
