Take care at your moment of triumph.<br />
When the lotus blooms brightest, it forgets the mud that nurtured it.<br />
Pride bloats the heart and blinds the mind.<br />
A king on his throne still stands upon the earth – a single crack in the ground can bring him down.<br />
Remember your beginnings when you reach the summit. Bow your head even at your highest, for the gods favor the modest.<br />
The mountain does not mock the valley; it knows that, with time, even stone wears away.
