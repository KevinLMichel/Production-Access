Avoid a senseless quarrel as you would avoid a cobra.<br />
Many angry words solve nothing and leave lasting wounds.<br />
If you disagree, speak calmly and listen deeply.<br />
Sometimes yielding a small point wins a greater peace.<br />
Do not pour oil on the fire of another’s rage; stay composed and gentle.<br />
Consider: is your goal to prove yourself right, or to find the truth?<br />
Pride makes us fight for an empty victory.<br />
Step back and let tempers cool – a dispute often softens with time and understanding.<br />
Wise are those who defer their wrath and seek common ground under Ma’at.
