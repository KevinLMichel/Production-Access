Be not rigid in your thinking – a tree that cannot bend will break in the storm.<br />
Welcome new ideas and even criticism, as the river welcomes tributaries and grows.<br />
Do not cling stubbornly to a course proven unwise; changing course can be wisdom, not weakness.<br />
The ostrich hides its head and sees nothing – do not be like it.<br />
Adapt to new truths as they reveal themselves. Stubborn pride in error serves no one.<br />
Hold your convictions firmly, but not so tightly that they choke out truth.<br />
Adjust your steps to stay in tune with Ma’at.<br />
The wise remain ever a student of the world.
