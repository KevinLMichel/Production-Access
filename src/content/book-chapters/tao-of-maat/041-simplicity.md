The sweetest water comes from a humble spring.<br />
Live simply, that you may live freely.<br />
Pride in fine garments and ornate palaces is a burden to the soul.<br />
Better a small hut where you laugh than a grand house where you cry.<br />
Possessions, once beyond need, begin to possess you.<br />
Let your heart be light and your footprint gentle on the earth.<br />
Owning little, you have nothing to fear losing.<br />
The wealth of a simple life is contentment and peace – treasures no thief can steal and no time can rust.
