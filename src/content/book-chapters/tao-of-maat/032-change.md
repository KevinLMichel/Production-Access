No state is eternal under the sun.<br />
Day turns to night, winter to spring, each in its season.<br />
Rejoice not overmuch in good fortune, nor despair too deeply in misfortune.<br />
The wheel of life turns, raising some up and bringing others low.<br />
If today you are strong, use your strength kindly, for tomorrow you may need kindness.<br />
If today you mourn, keep hope in your heart, for joy will find you again.<br />
Nothing is permanent except Ma’at itself – the order that brings change into meaning.<br />
Trust that order, and let each moment come and go like the tides.
