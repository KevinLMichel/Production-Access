When you embark on a worthy endeavor, persevere through difficulty.<br />
The stone is shaped to a statue by many steady strokes, not one blow.<br />
If you stumble, do not be ashamed – pick yourself up and continue.<br />
Obstacles test your resolve: the easy path often leads nowhere.<br />
Be patient as the eagle circling high, waiting for the right moment to strike.<br />
Great achievements are collections of small actions done faithfully each day.<br />
Do not let setbacks break your spirit. Rest if you must, but do not give up the journey.<br />
Ma’at favors the soul that keeps walking forward, even on rough ground.
