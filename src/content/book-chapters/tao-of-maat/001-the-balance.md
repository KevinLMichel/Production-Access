In the silence before dawn, the world hangs in perfect balance.<br />
Ma’at, the unseen order, renews itself each day – great and unshaken since the first sunrise.<br />
As the feather keeps the scale true, so does truth sustain sky and earth.<br />
Walk gently in this balance – neither at war with the world, nor idle within it –<br />
and you will find the path of Ma’at beneath your feet.
