Falsehood comforts for a moment, then crumbles.<br />
Truth is a foundation stone from first dawn.<br />
To live in truth is to live in light.<br />
Even if the light is gentle at times, it never wavers.<br />
A heart guided by truth fears no judgment; in the end, Ma’at’s feather finds it pure.
