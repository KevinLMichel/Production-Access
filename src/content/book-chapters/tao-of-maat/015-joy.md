Do not spend all your days in gloom, nor all in mirth.<br />
All toil and no laughter makes a life a burden; all play and no purpose leaves a life empty.<br />
Find joy in small blessings – a cool breeze at noon, a shared smile.<br />
Better a simple meal with a glad heart than a feast eaten in bitterness.<br />
Gratitude turns what you have into enough.<br />
When your heart is at peace with its portion, the gods themselves smile with you.
