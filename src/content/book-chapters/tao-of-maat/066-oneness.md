All things share one breath.<br />
Earth, sky, water, creatures, and we –<br />
currents within the same great river.<br />
Tug one current and the banks feel it;<br />
no joy or sorrow stands alone.<br />
See yourself in the stranger, the rival, the beast in the field.<br />
Harm another and the wound returns;<br />
help another and the blessing flows back.<br />
A drop is not apart from the river.<br />
Knowing this, walk gently; do no needless harm.<br />
Ma’at is the unity that holds the many as one.<br />
Live by Ma’at, and you honor all that is.
