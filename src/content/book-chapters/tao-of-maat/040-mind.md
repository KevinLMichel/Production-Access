As you think, so you become.<br />
The mind is a garden that can grow flowers or weeds.<br />
Nurture thoughts of compassion, fairness, and hope,<br />
and pluck out envy, fear, and despair as they sprout.<br />
What you allow to take root in your mind will bear fruit in your life.<br />
Do not dwell on anxious fantasies or bitter memories; let the present be your field of focus.<br />
Keep your thoughts aligned with Ma’at and you will know inner harmony.<br />
A balanced mind gives birth to a balanced life.
