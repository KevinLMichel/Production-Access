Life is sacred; treat it as such.<br />
When you approach the temple or the tomb, do so with a clean heart.<br />
Speak no blasphemy; make no oath lightly.<br />
The statues and symbols are stone and wood, but what they represent is eternal.<br />
Offer sincere prayers, not just ritual words.<br />
Honor the sacred in others – the divine spark in each heart.<br />
Reverence is not fear, but profound respect for what is greater than yourself.<br />
Walk gently under heaven’s gaze, and let your life be an offering of truth.
