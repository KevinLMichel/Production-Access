In the ruins of old tombs, wisdom is carved on stone.<br />
Heed the counsel of those who came before.<br />
They built the first roads; their failures and triumphs paved your way.<br />
By studying yesterday, you may foresee tomorrow.<br />
Emulate the best in your forebears and learn from their mistakes.<br />
Thus you honor their spirits and continue their story.<br />
The one who remembers their roots stands firm against the fiercest wind.
