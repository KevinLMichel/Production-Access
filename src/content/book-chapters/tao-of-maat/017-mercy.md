When wronged, the heart burns for revenge – but vengeance only doubles the bitterness.<br />
To forgive is to pour cool water over flames.<br />
It is the wise who mend a broken friendship while the foolish cling to pride.<br />
Justice does not forbid mercy: punish when you must, but do not let hatred poison you.<br />
By showing mercy to others, you heal your own soul.<br />
The one who forgives carries a lighter heart and walks closer to Ma’at.
