Judge not a person by fine clothes or high position.<br />
A heart of gold may beat under a tattered cloak, and a corrupt spirit may lurk behind a courtier’s smile.<br />
Outer beauty is fleeting; inner goodness is lasting.<br />
The true measure of a human is in their deeds and words, not in their ornaments.<br />
Do not be dazzled by titles or fooled by wealth.<br />
A clay cup holds pure water as well as a golden chalice.<br />
Honor virtue wherever it is found, even in the lowliest place.<br />
See others with Ma’at’s eyes, which peer straight into the heart.<br />
You will find truth often wears humble garb.
