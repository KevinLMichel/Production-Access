The world is a mirror; the deeds you send out will return to you.<br />
Show kindness and you invite kindness; deal cruelly and expect the same.<br />
Do not do to another what you would not want done to you.<br />
Your actions toward the weak and the stranger are the truest test of your character.<br />
If you seek love, give love. If you desire respect, show respect.<br />
The evil one does may seem to prosper at first, but in time misfortune finds its way home.<br />
Likewise, every generosity you scatter will bloom in unexpected places.<br />
All that you do circles back in time – walk accordingly under Ma’at’s eye.
