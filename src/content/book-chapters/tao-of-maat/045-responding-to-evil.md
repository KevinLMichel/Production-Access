If another does evil to you, do not mirror his deeds.<br />
To meet darkness with darkness only deepens the night.<br />
Resist the urge to strike back in kind; instead, stand firm in the light of Ma’at.<br />
Repay cruelty with fairness, and hatred with dignity.<br />
This is not surrender to wrong, but rising above it.<br />
By not becoming what you hate, you break the cycle.<br />
One who stays righteous in the face of wickedness is shielded by the unseen.<br />
In the contest between good and evil, one heart steadfast in good can tip the balance.
