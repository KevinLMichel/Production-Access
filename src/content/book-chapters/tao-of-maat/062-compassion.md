Treat the vulnerable with care and respect.<br />
Mock not the blind or the lame; dishonor not the old or the poor.<br />
How one treats the powerless is the measure of a heart.<br />
Remember, fortune’s wheel turns – tomorrow you might be the one in need.<br />
Extend a hand, not a sneer. The strong are meant to protect, not prey.<br />
All people carry the spark of life and deserve dignity.<br />
When you show compassion to those who suffer, you serve Ma’at itself.<br />
A gentle touch and a kind word can heal unseen wounds.<br />
By lifting another up, you rise as well in the eyes of eternity.
