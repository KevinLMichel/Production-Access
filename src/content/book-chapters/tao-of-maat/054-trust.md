Trust is a precious gift – give it wisely.<br />
Not everyone who smiles at you is your friend.<br />
It is good to trust in goodness, but also keep your eyes open.<br />
A serpent may hide under the sweetest flower.<br />
Yet a life without trust is a lonely field with no shelter.<br />
So trust, but test; forgive, but remember.<br />
Entrust your heart to those proven true.<br />
Even then, know that humans are fallible and may fail you.<br />
Place your ultimate trust in Ma’at, the eternal law of truth and balance, for it never falters.
