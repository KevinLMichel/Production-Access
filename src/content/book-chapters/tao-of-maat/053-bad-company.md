Choose carefully whom you keep near your fire.<br />
A quick-tempered friend will soon entangle you in needless flames.<br />
A thief will make you complicit in his thefts.<br />
One who laughs at injustice will dull your sense of right.<br />
You cannot wholly avoid the wicked, but you need not make them your companions.<br />
Better to walk alone than with a crowd running toward a cliff.<br />
Your character is reflected in the company you keep.<br />
Keep fellowship with the good, and even if trouble comes, you will not fall to disgrace.<br />
Ma’at is preserved when you surround yourself with those who honor it.
