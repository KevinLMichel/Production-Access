The cup that is overfilled spills on the ground.<br />
Take your portion and be satisfied.<br />
If you dine at a rich table, restrain your appetite; do not grasp at morsels with greedy hands.<br />
Excess is the enemy of well-being: the body bloated with indulgence houses a restless spirit.<br />
Eat until you are nourished, drink until your thirst is eased, and let contentment halt your hand.<br />
A small measure enjoyed in peace outlasts a banquet devoured in haste.
