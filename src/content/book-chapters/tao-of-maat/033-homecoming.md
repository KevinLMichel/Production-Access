A man fled his homeland when fear overtook him.<br />
In foreign lands he gained wealth and honor, yet each night his dreams were of the river and fields of his youth.<br />
No glory abroad could fill the void in his heart.<br />
At last he returned home, bowing before his rightful lord, and was received with mercy.<br />
Only then did he find the peace that had eluded him.<br />
The heart, like a bird, eventually flies back to where it belongs.<br />
Truth and belonging awaited him where he began.
