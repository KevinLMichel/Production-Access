No one lives forever, but a name can outlive the flesh.<br />
What will your name mean when it is spoken after you are gone?<br />
Monuments of stone crumble, but deeds of goodness remain in stories and hearts.<br />
Kindness to a stranger, wisdom shared with a child, justice done in secret – these are bricks in the house of your legacy.<br />
Writings may fade and statues fall, but the memory of virtue endures in the soul of the living.<br />
Live so that long after your departure, the echo of your life gives others courage.
