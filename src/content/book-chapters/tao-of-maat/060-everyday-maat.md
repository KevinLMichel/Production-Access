Ma’at lives not only in royal decrees or temple rites,<br />
but in the small daily choices of ordinary people.<br />
Each time you choose kindness over cruelty, honesty over comfort,<br />
you uphold Ma’at.

When you hold the door for a stranger, return a lost coin,<br />
or refuse to join in gossip, the feather of Ma’at is present.<br />
The strength of the world rests on countless small stones<br />
of justice and compassion.

Even unseen, your good acts set one block upon another.<br />
No righteous act is ever wasted, however humble.<br />
Live each day by Ma’at in little ways,<br />
and you become a living pillar of truth and balance.
