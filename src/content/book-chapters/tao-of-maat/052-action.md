Words are seeds; deeds are the fruit.<br />
A promise means little until it is fulfilled.<br />
Do not announce what you will do – do it, and let the work speak.<br />
The wind carries off idle boasts, but the results of effort remain.<br />
If you say you will help your brother, help him without delay.<br />
If you commit to a task, see it through.<br />
Many speak, few act; be among the few.<br />
In the end, actions – not words – uphold Ma’at in this world.
