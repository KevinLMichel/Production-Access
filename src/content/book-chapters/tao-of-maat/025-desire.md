Desire stirs like a great wind in the soul.<br />
If left unchecked, it can capsize the boat of reason.<br />
Acknowledge your desires, but steer them with a firm hand.<br />
Do not let fleeting pleasures lure you off the path of Ma’at.<br />
The sweet smell of the lotus can hide a pit of quicksand beneath.<br />
Enjoy life’s gifts in measure, without becoming their slave.<br />
Self-control is the staff that supports you on the long journey – with it in hand, you walk free of entanglement.
