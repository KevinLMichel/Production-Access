Do not be troubled if evildoers prosper for a time.<br />
Their shadows lengthen at evening; night will catch up to them.<br />
The hidden hand of heaven balances the scales when mortals cannot.<br />
An unjust man may sleep on silk, but his dreams are filled with jackals;<br />
a just man may sleep on straw, but his heart rests easy.<br />
Fret not at the delay of justice – the scales of heaven may shift slowly, but they settle true.<br />
In the end, every deed meets its due.<br />
Trust that Ma’at prevails beyond what your eyes can see.
