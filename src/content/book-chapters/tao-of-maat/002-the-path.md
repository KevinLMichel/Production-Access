Do not chase Ma’at with anxious hands; it is not a trophy to seize.<br />
Ma’at is a path unwinding under humble feet, a current carrying the unresisting heart.<br />
The harder you grasp the world, the more it slips away; the more gently you hold it, the more it supports you.<br />
Be as the river reed – yielding, yet rooted in truth –<br />
and you will outlast the flood.
