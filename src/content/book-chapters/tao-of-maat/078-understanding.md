Approach difference with curiosity, not judgment.<br />
The world is wide, and Ma’at embraces many faces.<br />
Scorn not what you do not understand.<br />
One man eats dates, another eats figs – both are nourished.

Hold to the truth as you see it, yet allow others their path if it brings no harm.<br />
Debate with civility; disagree without hatred.<br />
Often, beneath differing words and customs, the same heart beats.<br />
Find common ground where you can; where you cannot, offer respect.

Know that not everyone is your mirror – wisdom grows from that knowing.<br />
Unity is not uniformity; Ma’at builds harmony as many stones form one pyramid.
