The natural world is a scroll of divine teachings.<br />
Consider the stars: though distant and silent, they move in harmony each night.<br />
Consider the Nile: it rises and falls with patience, bringing life without asking praise.<br />
Trees give shade and fruit, asking nothing in return.<br />
Animals live by their design, not by malice or greed.<br />
Learn from these examples written by the Creator’s hand: live simply, give freely, take only what you need.<br />
If you care for the earth and its creatures, you keep Ma’at in your heart.<br />
All life breathes beneath the same sky – walk gently among your fellow beings.
