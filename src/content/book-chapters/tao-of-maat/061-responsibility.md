When things go wrong, the fool blames others, but the wise first examines themselves.<br />
Take responsibility for your actions and their consequences.<br />
If you have erred, admit it quickly and strive to mend the harm.<br />
Blame is easy and solves nothing.<br />
By owning your part in a problem, you gain the power to fix it.<br />
Life hands you circumstances, but how you meet them is your choice.<br />
Do not shirk duty or shift guilt.<br />
Stand upright in both success and failure.<br />
The load on your shoulders lightens when carried with honesty.<br />
Ma’at supports those who stand accountable under its gaze.
