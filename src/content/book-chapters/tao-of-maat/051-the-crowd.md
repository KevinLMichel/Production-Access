Do not simply follow the crowd; the path of the many is not always right.<br />
Think for yourself and stand by justice, even if you stand alone.<br />
Many hands can lift a load, but many voices can lead astray.<br />
The jackal pack may howl at the moon, but their noise does not change its course.<br />
Popularity is not the same as goodness.<br />
Let principle guide you above popularity.<br />
If others mock the truth, hold the truth tighter still.<br />
Often the path of Ma’at is a narrow trail in the wilderness – firm beneath the feet, though few find it.
