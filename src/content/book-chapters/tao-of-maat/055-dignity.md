When provoked by insults or foolish talk, keep your dignity.<br />
Flies cannot disturb calm water unless you let them.<br />
If a lesser man shouts, answer him with silence or measured words.<br />
To join his fury makes you his equal in folly.<br />
Know your worth, and do not lower yourself to wrestle in mud.<br />
There is strength in restraint: like an elephant beset by dogs, simply move on unmoved.<br />
All will see who is noble and who is the fool.<br />
In time, your composure will earn respect, while his fire burns to ash.
