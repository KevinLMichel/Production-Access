Your body is the vessel carrying your spirit through this journey.<br />
Treat it with respect and balance.<br />
Eat wholesome foods, but do not live to eat.<br />
Move your limbs so they remain strong, but let vanity not be your master.<br />
Avoid excesses that dull the mind or weaken the heart.<br />
Rest when you are weary – even the lion must sleep.<br />
Do not poison yourself with what you know will harm you.<br />
A clear mind dwells in a healthy body.<br />
By caring for your body, you honor the gift of life that Ma’at has given.
