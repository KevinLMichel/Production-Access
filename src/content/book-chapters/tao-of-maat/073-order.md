Ma’at thrives where order and respect prevail.<br />
Respect just laws, for they frame peace.<br />
Order is the vessel of truth.<br />
Play your part as a citizen upholding what is right.<br />
Defy not authority for pride’s sake, yet neither obey unjust commands.<br />
Offer honest counsel to those above if you can, but accept rightful decisions with grace.<br />
When each person keeps their duty in harmony, the whole kingdom prospers.<br />
Order is the vessel that carries truth – do not abandon it lightly.
