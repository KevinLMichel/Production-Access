Set counsel in the shrine within.<br />
In quiet, the heart speaks.<br />
Do not fear the lone hour.<br />
The desert at dusk teaches the willing.

When a storm of words rises,<br />
make a mooring post of your tongue.<br />
Sleep on your speech.<br />
Wind-swift talk passes. Truth remains.

Stand as the heron in still water.<br />
Let clarity gather like stars.<br />
Weigh your deeds and desires on the scale.<br />
In the hush, Ma’at steps from the clamor.<br />
Rise renewed, the heart set straight.
