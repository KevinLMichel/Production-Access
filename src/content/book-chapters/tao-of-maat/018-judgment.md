When sitting in judgment, be as an impartial scale.<br />
Listen to all sides before you decide; an answer given before hearing is a misshapen verdict.<br />
Anger and favoritism are weights that tip the balance – remove them.<br />
Let your mind be still and your heart steady.<br />
Judge as you would wish to be judged.<br />
Remember: all stand equal in the Hall of Truth when hearts are weighed.<br />
If you stray from justice now, your own heart will bear the weight later.
