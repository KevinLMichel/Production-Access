Beware the flatterer whose tongue drips honey but hides a knife.<br />
Not all who praise you seek your good; some feed you sweet words to serve their own ends.<br />
Value the friend who tells you a hard truth over the sycophant who tells you only what you wish to hear.<br />
A mirror that lies about your reflection is worse than no mirror at all.<br />
Do not let vanity deafen you to honest counsel.<br />
Likewise, be sparing with flattery yourself – give praise only when earned.<br />
Sincere words build trust; false praise builds traps.<br />
Walk in truth and require truth from those around you – that is armor against deception.
