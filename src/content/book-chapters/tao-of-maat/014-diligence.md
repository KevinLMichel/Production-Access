Perform your labor with a willing heart.<br />
The farmer who rises before dawn reaps a bountiful harvest, while the sluggard curses an empty field.<br />
Do your duty even when no eyes watch – Ma’at sees in secret and rewards the sincere.<br />
To work with skill is to honor the gifts you have been given.<br />
Do not disdain small tasks; a single brick laid each day builds a temple over years.<br />
Diligence in humble work is better than shirking a great duty.
