An idle hour is a treasure squandered.<br />
Fill your time with purpose, learning, or kindness – not with idle gossip and lounging.<br />
There is a time for rest, but let it be earned and brief.<br />
The lazy postpone every duty and find life slipping away empty.<br />
Work while it is day, so that when night comes you can sleep content.<br />
Do not say “I will do it tomorrow,” for too many tomorrows build only regret.<br />
The bee gathers nectar while it can; the Nile’s flood waits for no procrastinator.<br />
Use your time and energy wisely to avoid remorse.<br />
Diligence in youth brings comfort in old age; idleness brings only empty years.<br />
Ma’at favors the active, engaged heart.
