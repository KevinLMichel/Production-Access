No matter how far you have gone down a wrong road, you can always turn back.<br />
Each morning the sun returns and with it a chance to begin anew.<br />
Do not cling to past mistakes out of pride or despair.<br />
Shed your old skin like a serpent and step again onto the path of Ma’at.<br />
The gods are patient with those who sincerely change.<br />
Apologize where needed, forgive yourself, and move forward.<br />
As long as breath remains, hope remains for a better course.<br />
A tarnished heart can be polished by honest effort.<br />
Renewal is always possible – this mercy is woven into creation.
