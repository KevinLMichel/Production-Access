In both victory and defeat, carry yourself with honor.<br />
Win without boasting, lose without bitterness.<br />
One day’s outcome does not define a life – Ma’at weighs the total of your heart, not a single battle.<br />
Help your fallen rival to his feet; there is more nobility in mercy than in triumph.<br />
Do not delight in your enemy’s downfall, lest the gods see and judge you by the same measure.<br />
True honor is not in never falling, but in rising without malice each time you fall.<br />
Your character in dark times is the test of your light.
