Fear is a shadow that shrinks the heart.<br />
Some fear is natural and keeps us safe; too much fear binds us like a prison.<br />
To live rightly, one must sometimes walk through fear as through a dark tunnel, trusting light ahead.<br />
Courage is not the absence of fear, but action in spite of it.<br />
Call upon Ma’at when your knees tremble – remember the justice and truth you stand for.<br />
Better to fail doing right than to succeed through cowardice.<br />
A lion may sleep in your heart; rouse it when needed.<br />
Each act of courage feeds the soul, and fear flees from a steadfast heart.
