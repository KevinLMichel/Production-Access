No one is an island entire of itself.<br />
As villagers join to raise a house, so we need one another.<br />
Offer help freely to your neighbors, and accept help humbly in turn.<br />
Do not isolate yourself in pride or fear.<br />
Sharing burdens makes them lighter; sharing joys makes them brighter.<br />
A community in harmony is like a boat crew rowing in time: their journey is swift and smooth.<br />
Contribute your strength to the common good, and you strengthen yourself as well.<br />
Weave yourself gently into the lives of others, and you will find Ma’at in the bonds between hearts.
