Let gratitude be your daily ritual.<br />
At sunrise, give thanks for the light; at night, give thanks for rest.<br />
The grateful heart turns even modest possessions into abundant blessings.<br />
Do not focus on what is missing – cherish what is present.<br />
Complaints furrow the face and invite bitterness to take root.<br />
One who counts their blessings finds joy multiplied.<br />
Thankfulness is wine for the soul: drink it and see the world shine.<br />
Even in hardship, find one thing to be thankful for; that spark will light your way out of darkness.<br />
A grateful heart stays in balance with Ma’at.
