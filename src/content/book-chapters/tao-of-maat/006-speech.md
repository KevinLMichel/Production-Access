Words can warm, words can burn.<br />
A kind word can nourish a heart for years; a cruel word can scar it for a lifetime.<br />
Before you let words fly, weigh them on the scale of your heart – will they build, or will they burn?<br />
Speak plainly and with care.<br />
Let your tongue be a tool to heal, not a dagger to wound.<br />
In the mouth of the wise, words are a salve; in the mouth of the reckless, a poison.
