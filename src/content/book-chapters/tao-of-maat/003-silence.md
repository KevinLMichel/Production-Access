Words have fire; silence has light.<br />
In a council of noise, the quiet one hears clearly.<br />
The fool’s voice is loud, declaring his own wisdom;<br />
the sage’s voice is soft, asking to learn.<br />
Be swift to listen, slow to proclaim.<br />
Like a deep well, the still heart reflects the sky,<br />
while the agitated heart churns up only mud.
