As the sun takes time to climb the sky, so good things come in their season.<br />
Rushing a task spoils its fruit; impatience is a drought that cracks the soul’s soil.<br />
Wait for the right moment, as the farmer waits for the flood to recede before sowing.<br />
To be patient is not to do nothing, but to do what is right at the right pace.<br />
Ma’at rewards the heart that can wait without wavering.
