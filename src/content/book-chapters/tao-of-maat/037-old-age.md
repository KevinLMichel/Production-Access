Age creeps upon us like the slow rising of the Nile.<br />
One day you find your hair a river of gray and your back bent like a bow.<br />
Do not lament getting older – each year is a harvest of lessons.<br />
In youth one has vigor; in age one gains perspective.<br />
Respect the elderly, for they have traveled roads you have yet to walk.<br />
As you age, do not cling to youth’s glories; embrace the role of guide and storyteller.<br />
A sunset has its own beauty no sunrise can rival.<br />
In life’s cycle, old age is not an end but the season of wisdom.
