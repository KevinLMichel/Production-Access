Be the same person in darkness as you are in light.<br />
Let integrity be a straight rod that does not bend in the wind.<br />
If offered a bribe, reject it as you would a cup of poison.<br />
Ill-gotten gains turn to dust, while a clean conscience is a soft pillow.<br />
Speak truth even when it costs you, and do right even when none see.<br />
The true judge of your deeds is the heart within.<br />
Keep your integrity, and your heart will rest easy under the stars.
