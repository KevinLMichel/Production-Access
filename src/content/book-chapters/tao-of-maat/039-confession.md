To admit a fault is the first step to healing.<br />
Even the righteous heart errs at times.<br />
Do not persist in a lie to save face – hidden rot only spreads unseen.<br />
Stand before your own heart and speak the truth of your deeds.<br />
Ask forgiveness where you have harmed, and make right what you can.<br />
The gods know we are not perfect; they value the sincere, not the falsely perfect.<br />
In the Hall of Truth, better to have stumbled and confessed<br />
than to wear a mask of virtue over a corrupted heart.
