Grief comes to all who love – it is the price of having a full heart.<br />
Do not be ashamed of your tears for those you have lost; your tears honor them.<br />
But do not let sorrow become your master.<br />
The dead do not desire endless mourning; they have gone west, awaiting your smile again.<br />
Speak of your loved ones with joy for the time you shared.<br />
By remembering their deeds and speaking their names, you give them life among the living.<br />
Your heart will heal in time, as the lotus closes at night and opens with the sun.<br />
Ma’at teaches that all cycles – death to life, sorrow to hope.<br />
Trust that the sun will rise even after the darkest night of grief.
