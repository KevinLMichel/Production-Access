There are things in life you can change and things you cannot.<br />
Wisdom is knowing one from the other.<br />
After you have planted and tended, accept the field’s yield.<br />
After you have done your part in justice, accept that some outcomes lie with Ma’at and the gods.<br />
Rail not against the inevitable – storms will come despite prayer; loved ones will depart despite tears.<br />
Meet what you cannot alter with grace.<br />
Acceptance is not surrender of goodness, but surrender of futile struggle.<br />
By accepting what you cannot control, you find peace amid change.<br />
In acceptance, your heart remains unbroken by the weight of the world.
