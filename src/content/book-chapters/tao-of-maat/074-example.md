Do not demand of others what you refuse to do yourself.<br />
Rule yourself before you rule anyone else.<br />
An unruly house cannot lead a village, just as an undisciplined person cannot guide others.<br />
Lead by example, not by force.<br />
If you want your children or students to be virtuous, show them virtue in your own life.<br />
Correct your own faults before pointing out your neighbor’s.<br />
People are persuaded more by a life of honor than by a hundred loud proclamations.<br />
Be to others a living model of Ma’at.<br />
In your actions, let others see truth and balance, and they will be inspired to follow.
