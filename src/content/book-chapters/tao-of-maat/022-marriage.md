When you take a partner in life, let love and fidelity guide you.<br />
Treat your spouse with reverence and kindness, as your own heart.<br />
Do not dominate, nor neglect – a household thrives on mutual care.<br />
Provide for your companion’s needs and delight in their happiness.<br />
A marriage founded on trust is a boat that can sail any river.<br />
When there is disagreement, speak without scorn and listen without defense.<br />
Two hearts in harmony create an enduring home.
