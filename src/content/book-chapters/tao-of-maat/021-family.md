Honor those who gave you life.<br />
Respect your father’s wisdom and your mother’s love – these were your first guides.<br />
Nurture your children with gentleness and good counsel; they carry your name into the future.<br />
Quarrel not with siblings over trifles; a family united can weather any storm.<br />
A home built on love and respect is a fortress no wind can overturn.<br />
In the evening of life, the memory of harmony at home is a treasure beyond gold.
