Imagine the scales in the Hall of Truth: on one side, your heart; on the other, the feather of Ma’at.<br />
A heavy heart, laden with wrongdoing, tips the balance against you.<br />
A light heart, free of malice and regret, rises and passes the test.<br />
Each day, weigh your own heart in quiet reflection.<br />
Remove what weighs it down – anger, deceit, greed – one by one.<br />
Feed it virtues that make it feather-light – kindness, honesty, peace.<br />
Do this, and when the final judgment comes you will have no fear.<br />
Your heart will be light as the feather of Ma’at, and you will pass into the Field of Reeds justified.
