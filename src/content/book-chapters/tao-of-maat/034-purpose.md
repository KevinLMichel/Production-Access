Each heart beats with a purpose set by the divine.<br />
Do not ignore the calling that makes your spirit sing.<br />
If your gift is to craft, then craft with devotion; if to heal, then heal with compassion.<br />
A life without purpose is a ship without a rudder – adrift and aimless.<br />
Time is the most precious gift; wasting it is an insult to the spirit.<br />
Work at what brings you and others real good.<br />
Thus when your days draw to a close, you will rest content,<br />
knowing you lived true to the song in your heart.
