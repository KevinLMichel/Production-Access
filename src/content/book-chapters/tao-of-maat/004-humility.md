Do not let knowledge swell the heart.<br />
No person knows every secret; we are all students under the sky.<br />
Listen for wisdom in every voice – a peasant’s truth can surpass a prince’s folly.<br />
The river of knowledge has many sources: clear water may spring from a humble well.<br />
Bend your ear low to the ground of life; the seeds of insight often sprout in unlikely soil.
