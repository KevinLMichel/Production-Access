Seek advice from the wise who have walked the road before you.<br />
Pride can deafen us to good guidance.<br />
Even a king needs counsel; even a sage listens to another voice.<br />
Consider the counsel of the experienced, then decide your course.<br />
Do not be ashamed to ask directions when lost – the road to truth is found by seeking.<br />
Surround yourself with honest advisers, not merely those who agree with you.<br />
Listen also to the counsel of your own conscience in quiet moments.<br />
With many counselors, plans are strengthened; with none, they falter.<br />
Heed good counsel and you add the knowledge of others to your own.
