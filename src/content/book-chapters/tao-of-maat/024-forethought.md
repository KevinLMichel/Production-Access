The wise plan for tomorrow even as they live fully today.<br />
Before you build, envision the house; before you speak, imagine the echo.<br />
A moment of thought can prevent an hour of regret.<br />
Foresight is a lamp in the darkness of uncertainty.<br />
Do not fear to dream and design, but also prepare for the unexpected storm.<br />
Let your plans be as flexible as the river’s course – bending around obstacles, yet ever flowing toward its goal.<br />
By planting seeds of forethought, you harvest fields of peace.
