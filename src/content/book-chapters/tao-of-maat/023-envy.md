Chasing another man’s star will only lead you astray.<br />
Envy is a shadow that clouds the eye, making sweet things taste bitter.<br />
Rejoice in your own blessings and they will grow.<br />
The fruit on your neighbor’s tree may look golden, but every garden has its hidden thorns.<br />
By coveting what is not yours, you invite sorrow into your heart.<br />
Keep your gaze on your own path – Ma’at has given you what you need for your journey.
