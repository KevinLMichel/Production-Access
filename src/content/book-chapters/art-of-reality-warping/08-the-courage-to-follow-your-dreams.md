## The Power of Forgiveness
From deep within the soul,<br />
A voice calls out in pain.<br />
The past still haunts, takes its toll,<br />
These nightmares give us strain.<br />
You have some courage to forgive,<br />
To let go of what's been done,<br />
A forgiving heart begins to live,<br />
And finds a new dawn has begun.<br />
Forgiving frees the mind,<br />
From anger's endless hold.<br />
And peace of heart we find,<br />
And peaceful dreams need not control.<br />
Do take the courage to forgive,<br />
Break the chains of pain.<br />
Find a new life to live,<br />
A new hope to sustain.<br />

## The Wonder of Creation
From stars we came, to stars we'll go,<br />
The journey of life a mystery untold,<br />
Yet in every moment, big and small,<br />
A beauty lies waiting, ready to enthrall.<br />
With every dream that takes flight,<br />
And every step we dare to take,<br />
A world of wonder comes alive,<br />
Our imagination, the world to make.<br />
With courage in our hearts,<br />
And wonder in our eyes,<br />
We reach for stars and dreams,<br />
And claim the skies.<br />
From every seed that's sown,<br />
To every tree that grows,<br />
There's magic in the earth,<br />
And beauty in the flows.<br />
For every act of creation,<br />
Is a symphony of art,<br />
With every stroke, a declaration,<br />
Shows the wonder of healed heart.<br />

## The Beauty of Nature
Amidst the rush of life, where time and worries swirl,<br />
There lies a world of peace, a beauty to unfurl.<br />
In every blade of grass,<br />
In every ripple of the sea,<br />
There's magic in the world,<br />
And wonder there to be.<br />
Seems nature is the mirror, of much that's true and bright,<br />
Reflecting so much beauty, see you, now in full flight!<br />
With courage in resilient heart,<br />
And wonder in your eyes,<br />
Now take in nature's art,<br />
As you reach and touch majestic skies.<br />
In every sunrise, every starry night,<br />
Magic in the air, beauty is the sight.<br />
Mountains high,<br />
Seas dark blue,<br />
Magic in the earth,<br />
Beauty, that is you!<br />
Nature is your guide, watch its beauty in the warm light,<br />
Therefrom the earth and sky, emerged your courage to take flight.<br />
