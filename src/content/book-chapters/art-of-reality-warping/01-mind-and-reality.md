## The Power of Perception
Thought wields a mighty force, warping our very sight,<br />
A lens to guide our vision through the darkness of the night.<br />
Our worlds, they pulse and swirl with life, alive and breathing things,<br />
And each new moment holds the power to shape the life that springs.<br />
With every thought, we weave a tapestry anew,<br />
A world that molds to our desires, gleaming bright and true.<br />
And in this power, we grasp a key to change and growth,<br />
To shatter limits, shift perceptions, and leave old ways for both.<br />
The mind's potential to craft a reality,<br />
Is a gift we must use with humility and generosity.<br />
For as we alter thought, so too our lives transform,<br />
In joy, abundance, grace, and beauty, to conform.<br />
Thus, let us embrace this power, with all our will and might,<br />
For in it lies a key to see anew the world's delight.<br />
To shape our destinies, and make our dreams take flight,<br />
With perceptive strength, and insight's shining light.<br />

## The Illusion of Separation
We cut ourselves from one another, with a blindness, born of fear,<br />
A notion that we stand alone, in this life, so dear.<br />
A world that seems so distant, and so vastly, hard to see,<br />
Yet, it all exists within us, in a reality we can make be.<br />
We warp our own perceptions, with the facts that we believe,<br />
A fallacy of separateness, that stops what we could receive.<br />
The love, the grace, the beauty, that surrounds us every day,<br />
Yet, we shut it out, and in so doing, our selves we do betray.<br />
The truth that we are all one, in a cosmic tapestry,<br />
A grand design, where every thread, is woven artfully.<br />
And in this unity, we find a sense of peace and calm,<br />
That juxtaposes all the fear that leaves us feeling so alarmed.<br />
We must get out, we must break free, from the delusions we create,<br />
And see the world for what it is, with a vision, oh, so great!<br />
For in that world, we can find a love so bright and warm,<br />
That will light our way, and lead us to a joy amidst the storm.<br />

## The Nature of Thought
The mind be it so curious, with a power that is divine,<br />
A force that can shape the world, a force to make it thine,<br />
Its nature is strange, a mystery foretold,<br />
Vast and endless in its space, thoughts therein unfold.<br />
You shall use this knowledge, and be bold,<br />
To shape your thoughts, and to reach your goal,<br />
For in the nature of your mind, you'll find,<br />
The power to change the world while being kind.<br />
The mind is a tool, so use it right,<br />
Achieve your dreams, pursue your light,<br />
With each held thought you change your fate,<br />
Use your mind-tear down the gates.<br />
