A journey of the soul,<br />
Where we are free to be.<br />

The soil as if your feet,<br />
Every step you take,<br />

Just take the leap, embrace the change,<br />
Understand where the tide leads,<br />

## The Importance of Self-Care
When your mind and soul are weighed down,<br />
By life's most arduous challenges and cares,<br />
Do not forget to cherish your own crown,<br />
And show your body kindness and repairs.<br />
For within yourself, lies the source of your power,<br />
That gives you strength to withstand life's rough ride,<br />
With self-care, you'll find your spirit can flower,<br />
And bloom with happiness and peace inside.<br />
Take time to breathe, to pause and to reflect,<br />
And tune into your body and your mind,<br />
To nourish your soul, and to neglect,<br />
The worries that keep you from peace confined.<br />
Through mindful breathing, you'll warp your reality,<br />
And find new ways to love and care for self,<br />
For when you tend to your own vitality,<br />
You'll give yourself the gift of inner wealth.<br />
So listen to your heart and to your soul,<br />
And trust in your journey of self-discovery,<br />
For it is within yourself, you'll find your goal,<br />
And the key to lasting happiness and glory.<br />
