## The Meaning of Success
Success, a word so easily spoken<br />
But its meaning, often, broken<br />
Some measure it by wealth untold<br />
Others, by power, fame and gold.<br />
But what is success, truly and fair?<br />
Is it a destination, or a journey to share?<br />
For it's not the end, but the path we take<br />
That defines our success, for goodness' sake!<br />
We march on, driven by a hunger inside<br />
With purpose and passion, we dare to stride<br />
Along roads that are rocky, amidst winds that are strong,<br />
With persistence and determination, we do march on.<br />
We chase after success, with a single goal,<br />
And in its pursuit, we surrender our soul.<br />
Do we ignore the process, and not see its worth,<br />
Shall we measure our lives just by wealth and mirth?<br />
Maybe success must include these material things?<br />
We could use well the money, and the fame that it brings?<br />
So too success is the journey and the state of mind and heart,<br />
A feeling of peace, a good life that's pure art.<br />
It's not just the finish line, though you can cross it with pride,<br />
It's not just the trophies, but still take those in stride,<br />
It's a process, a journey, there in each step you take,<br />
It's motivating energy, not the anti-climax that awaits.<br />
Embrace the process-love the journey, and all that it holds,<br />
And see success, in the stories that you have told.<br />
Success is not just one word, success is a massive relentless way,<br />
Of living a life, each day, come what may.<br />

## The Importance of Kindness
Kindness, a virtue so rare, so pure,<br />
A gentle spirit, with a power to endure.<br />
Warping the world, with its soft embrace<br />
Bringing comfort, to even the harshest place.<br />
In the grand scheme of things, it may seem small,<br />
But like a pebble thrown, its ripples enthrall.<br />
Each act of kindness, a seed planted in time,<br />
Growing, spreading, to change the ill some may find.<br />
For kindness is not just a word, nor simply a deed,<br />
It's a force that ignites, it's a spark for a seed.<br />
A wave that spreads, and warps the darkness of night,<br />
Into a brilliant dawn, of hope, and new light.<br />
It is the hand that lifts, and the heart that lends,<br />
A helping hand, in the times that call for a friend.<br />
It is the smile, that lifts the spirit so high,<br />
And warps the world, into a better place, where love can thrive.<br />

## The Power of Mindfulness
In this fast-paced world, it's all too easy to get caught up<br />
In the rush of daily life, where worries and fears erupt.<br />
But the mind can be tamed, like a horse to a rein;<br />
With mindfulness, our thoughts can be harnessed and trained.<br />
The power of mindfulness goes beyond just a peaceful mind;<br />
It helps us see beyond the veil of what society has designed<br />
To shape our thoughts, our actions, and our reality,<br />
To live life fully, with purpose and in clarity.<br />
We can cultivate this power through meditation and grace,<br />
And use it to craft compassion, love, and acceptance in our chosen place,<br />
To live life in the moment and cherish each sensation,<br />
And find meaning in our journey, in a world filled with distraction.<br />
By being mindful of our thoughts, we gain a newfound might,<br />
A power to transform our world and shape our own light.<br />
We can learn to be present, to live life with purpose and drive,<br />
And experience true joy and peace that only mindfulness can provide.<br />
