## Chapter Promise
Expose the theater of legitimacy.

## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 1,000 words.

## Scene Architecture
I enter a cathedral where priests of seriousness bless certain ideas and condemn others.

Blessed as realistic:

bombing

austerity

surveillance

debt

layoffs

tax incentives

permanent emergency

Condemned as naïve:

housing everyone

healing everyone

shortening work

educating without debt

asking who owns the future

## Key Narrative Beats
- I kneel because everyone else kneels.
- A priest offers communion in the form of a slogan.
- I refuse to swallow it.
- The congregation turns.
- The priest says, “You must be practical.”
- I ask, “Practical for whom?”
- A stained-glass window cracks.

## Narrator Movement
Refusal becomes a stronger form of agency.

## Transition
The cracked stained glass falls as coupons.
