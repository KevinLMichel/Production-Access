## Chapter Promise
Agency through inquiry. The world responds to a forbidden question.

## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 1,200 words.

## Scene Architecture
The exam rewards compliance. Questions include:

“Which answer is safest?”

“Which authority must not be questioned?”

“How many facts are necessary before curiosity becomes disorder?”

“Select the correct reason why nothing can change.”

## Key Narrative Beats
- Students who answer quickly receive medals.
- Students who hesitate receive debt slips.
- A girl writes her own question in the margin.
- The proctor marks it wrong.
- I take her paper and read the question aloud: “Who decided this was the test?”
- The desks shift.
- The room widens.

## Transition
The girl’s pencil becomes a hammer.
