## Chapter Promise
Give the book its central inferno. This must be concrete and unbearable without becoming exploitative.

## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 1,250 words.

## Scene Architecture
Stalingrad appears as a city of white buildings, gardens, factories, and smoke. Air-raid loudspeakers speak calmly. People do not believe them at first because they have heard warnings before.

Then the bombing begins.

## Key Narrative Beats
- I see picnickers on a hill look up.
- A mother drops a basket of apples.
- Bombs fall in orderly rows.
- Wooden houses burn, leaving chimneys like grave markers.
- Apartment blocks remain standing but hollowed.
- A hospital is hit; children are thrown from beds.
- Oil tanks ignite; the river burns.

## Craft Constraint
Keep the narrator mobile but not heroic. He sees, helps briefly, fails often.

## Transition
I run toward the river carrying a child’s shoe. The river is on fire.
