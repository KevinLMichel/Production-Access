## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 1,200 words.

## Scene Architecture
I enter a circular court. No judge presides. Present are workers, soldiers, children, civilians, nurses, teachers, engineers, programmers, prisoners, the dead of Stalingrad, the hungry of the supermarket, the students from the classroom, and the unborn.

The court waits.

## Key Narrative Beats
- Each group brings an object: a wrench, a burned shoe, a report card, a rifle bolt, a prescription, a timecard, a broken screen, a toy.
- No one accuses me directly.
- That is worse.
- A child asks me what I helped build.
- I cannot answer quickly.
- The silence becomes evidence.

## Narrator Movement
I must stop treating myself as only a spectator.

## Transition
The center of the court opens. The factory machine rises.
