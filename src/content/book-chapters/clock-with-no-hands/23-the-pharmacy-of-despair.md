## Scene Architecture
At the back of the supermarket is a pharmacy that resembles a chapel. People kneel before counters. Prescriptions are priced according to hope.

## Key Narrative Beats
- A man asks for insulin and is offered a payment plan.
- A woman asks for sleep and receives a bottle labeled “resilience.”
- A child asks why his father drinks.
- The pharmacist says, “Supply chain.”
- Behind the counter, executives dine.
- I climb over the counter.
- The pharmacist tells me customers are not allowed back there.
- I say I am not a customer.
- The lights flicker.

## Transition
A pill bottle rolls off the counter and becomes a robot’s eye.
