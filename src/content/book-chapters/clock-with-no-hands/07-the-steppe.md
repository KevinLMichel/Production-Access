## Chapter Promise
Move from abstract machinery to historical machinery.

## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 950 words.

## Scene Architecture
The factory floor is now a tank tread moving over a vast steppe. I stand on the tank as if on a train. Dust becomes ash. Ash becomes snow. The horizon glows.

Men around me are young, dirty, confident, afraid. They speak of reaching a river. One says the war will be over soon. Another writes a letter home in which he lies gently.

## Key Narrative Beats
- I see maps spread over ammunition crates.
- The map shows arrows entering empty space.
- The empty space begins to bleed through the paper.
- Someone says, “Nothing is impossible.”
- A horse collapses beside a fuel truck.
- No one has time to mourn it.

## Transition
Smoke columns rise ahead. The tank slows. The city appears.
