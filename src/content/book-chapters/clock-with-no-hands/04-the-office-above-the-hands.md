## Chapter Promise
Show calculation without caricature. The horror is bureaucratic calm.

## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 1,000 words.

## Scene Architecture
I climb a staircase to a glass office suspended above the factory. Inside sit executives, generals, ministers, economists, consultants, and priests of efficiency. They speak calmly in abstractions.

Below them, hands move. Above them, mouths speak.

## Key Narrative Beats
- A chart shows “human hours” converted into “shareholder light.”
- A man in a clean shirt says, “Pain is not counted unless it appears in the column.”
- Another says, “The column is civilization.”
- I ask where the products go.
- They point upward.
- I look up and see a dark ceiling covered with hooks.

## Craft Constraint
The office should not feel like a villain lair. It should feel sterile, courteous, credentialed.

## Transition
One executive stamps a paper. The stamp becomes an artillery impact.
