## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 1,150 words.

## Scene Architecture
The classroom opens into a library that resembles the internet: endless shelves, screens, whispers, fragments, archives, rumors, maps, charts, manifestos, recipes, propaganda, love letters, casualty lists.

## Key Narrative Beats
- People collect disconnected facts in baskets.
- Some facts attract each other and form monsters.
- Others form bridges.
- A librarian tells me access is not understanding.
- I ask for the most important book.
- She hands me a blank one titled WHAT ARE YOU LOOKING FOR?
- Words appear only when I write a question.

## Narrator Movement
I become capable of generating doors through questions.

## Transition
The library shelves rearrange into rows of television screens.
