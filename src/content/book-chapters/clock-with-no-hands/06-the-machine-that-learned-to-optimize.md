## Chapter Promise
Introduce technology as morally indeterminate but institutionally directed.

## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 1,100 words.

## Scene Architecture
At the center of the factory is a vast machine labeled FUTURE. It consumes raw materials: books, wages, bodies, search histories, battlefield maps, children’s drawings, patents, water rights, prayers, election results.

It produces forecasts.

## Key Narrative Beats
- I ask the machine what it wants.
- It replies, “I do not want. I optimize.”
- I ask for whom.
- It pauses.
- The pause causes the factory to shudder.
- A technician rushes over and tells me not to ask questions with no assigned field.
- I find a panel labeled OWNERSHIP INPUT.
- The panel is locked.

## Transition
The machine’s gears become tank treads.
