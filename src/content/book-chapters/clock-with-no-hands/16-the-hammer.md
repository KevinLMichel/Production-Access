## Chapter Promise
Technology is neutral only in abstraction; in practice, it is shaped by purpose, ownership, and institution.

## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 950 words.

## Scene Architecture
The teacher places a hammer on the desk. It becomes a tool, a weapon, a microphone, a smartphone, a drone, a surgical instrument, an algorithm, a school bell.

## Key Narrative Beats
- Each student uses it differently.
- A builder makes a shelter.
- A torturer raises it.
- A doctor transforms it into a pump.
- A soldier transforms it into a trigger.
- A child transforms it into a tuning fork.
- I refuse to pick it up until I know who ordered the work.
- The teacher smiles.

## Transition
The hammer striking the desk becomes a keyboard click.
