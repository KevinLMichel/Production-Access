## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 1,100 words.

## Scene Architecture
In the city center, a clock hangs on a ruined wall. Its hands are made of bone. Every tick corresponds to a body falling somewhere nearby.

## Key Narrative Beats
- Officers argue over minutes.
- Soldiers measure distance in corpses.
- A messenger arrives too late because a street no longer exists.
- I try to stop the clock.
- The bone hand cuts my palm.
- My blood falls onto the dial.
- For one second the ticking stops.
- Everyone looks up.
- Then someone shouts and the battle resumes.

## Transition
Blood from my palm falls through the floor and becomes red ink on an exam paper.
