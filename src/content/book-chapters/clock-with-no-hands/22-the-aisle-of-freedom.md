## Scene Architecture

## Key Narrative Beats

- A student buys credentials with years of future labor.
- A worker buys groceries and pays with sleep.
- A veteran tries to use a medal as currency.
- The cashier says, “We do not accept honor.”
- A sign flashes: RECORD PROFITS / PLEASE BE PATIENT WITH SHORTAGES.
