## Chapter Promise
Dramatize alienation as architecture.

## Draft Frame
Final prose target: 1,300 words.

## Scene Architecture
This chapter is structured as four vignettes, each following one worker.

Worker one: product separationA carpenter builds doors for houses he will never enter. Every door he finishes locks from the other side.

Worker two: activity separationA woman performs the same movement at a machine. Her wrist becomes a metronome. She hums a song but must stop because rhythm lowers efficiency.

Worker three: self separationA young man in a headset repeats a script until his own name sounds foreign.

Worker four: social separationTwo workers on adjacent stations are evaluated against each other. Each believes the other is the obstacle.

## Key Narrative Beats
- I try to return one finished object to the worker who made it.
- The object rejects her hand, as if magnetized upward.
- I try to speak to two workers at once, but a transparent wall slides between them.
- I place my palm on the wall. It vibrates with advertisements.

## Narrator Movement
My interventions fail, but the world reacts.

## Transition
The transparent wall becomes a television screen showing a motivational training video.
