This work is the first letter to my friend Aloysius regarding the “Virtue of the Gods.” It has been made available to you the reader, so that you may also benefit. In the letter, I discuss the first virtue required to be one with the gods - a god walks through fire! The reader may be inspired to assume his/her true nature as a divine being. The reader may come to see the responsibility which he/she has to create a heaven on Earth, and throughout this universe.
