I must return to other affairs Aloysius, yet I shall write you again soon with more exploration of this issue. I wish you confidence, and focus, as you walk through your fire - for you create your own fire Aloysius; you judge yourself. You are the god of fire! Are you sadistic or merciful? Are you able to help, and lift up, and help reform you fellow man, or do you just cast him away, label him hopeless, and burn him? For verily, I say onto you, as ye judge your fellow man, so shall you be judged.

Aloysius, I wish you the virtues of the gods. We shall correspond again soon, until then, farewell.
