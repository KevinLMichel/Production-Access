Wallace D. Wattles (1860 - 1911) was a pioneer of the New Thought movement, and his influence still resonates in modern success teachings. Even a century later, his ideas continue to change lives—The Science of Getting Rich was famously credited in The Secret as a life - altering inspiration. Wattles’ direct, no - nonsense style and practical spiritual philosophy have guided countless readers to achieve their aims by “thinking in a Certain Way” and acting with purpose. His classic trilogy (The Science of Getting Rich, The Science of Being Well, and The Science of Being Great) laid out methods for attaining wealth, health, and personal greatness through alignment with what he called the Formless Intelligence (the creative power of the universe).

In his writings, Wattles hinted that acquiring the right mindset was only part of the equation; inspired action was the other essential component of creation. This book, The Science of Inspired Action, picks up that thread and carries his voice forward. It is written in the spirit of Wallace D. Wattles—indeed, in his very voice—to explore how we can harness the power of inspired, purposeful action in our lives. The subtitle, “The Return of Wallace D. Wattles,” reflects the aim of this project: to bring Wattles’ perspective back to readers, as if he himself were here again to impart new wisdom.

Following this Introduction is a Foreword written in Wattles’ voice (channeled through me), where he “returns” to set the stage. After the Foreword, the book is organized into eight chapters, each delving into an aspect of acting in harmony with creative intelligence. These chapters—from The Right to Act Greatly to The Law of Timely Sufficiency—build on one another to form a comprehensive guide. Each chapter concludes with Immediate Action bullet points, offering practical takeaways so you can begin applying Wattles’ principles right now.

My goal as the author has been to remain as true as possible to Wattles’ methodology and tone. I have studied his work deeply, and in these pages I step aside to let his wisdom speak through. The Science of Inspired Action is meant as a living extension of Wattles’ philosophy. I hope you will feel Wallace D. Wattles himself guiding you as you read. May this “return” of his voice inspire you to take the kind of action that transforms your life in powerful ways.

Foreword by Wallace D. Wattles (Channeled through Kevin L. Michel)

I speak to you now across the years, returning with a message that burns bright in my soul. Though more than a century has passed since I first put pen to paper, truth is timeless, and the laws of life and success have not changed. It is with joy and purpose that I “return” to elaborate on a key idea from my earlier teachings — the principle of Inspired Action.

In my life’s work, I taught the science of getting rich, of being well, and of being great. I showed that by thinking in a certain way—by forming a clear vision, keeping an unshakable faith, and maintaining deep gratitude—one could invoke the creative power of the universe to achieve one’s desires. But I also taught that thought alone is not enough. By thought, the thing you want is brought to you; by action, you receive it. Now I will guide you more deeply into that action, so that your every step is taken in unity with the Infinite Intelligence that lies within and around you.

I return now because the world still yearns for a clearer understanding of how to move from inspired idea to tangible result. Many people hold good thoughts and earnest desires, yet falter when it comes to doing. Some rush about without direction, while others hesitate despite the spark within urging them forward. My aim here is to help you recognize and trust that inner spark, and to act upon it effectively. Inspired Action is action aligned with the creative Spirit — doing the right thing at the right time with an inner knowing born of the higher mind. When you learn to act in this way, you cannot help but advance in life.

In the chapters ahead, I will speak to you about the art and science of acting in harmony with your divine purpose. We will affirm your right to act greatly, explore the laws that govern successful action, and lay out practical steps to recognize the pulse of inspiration and harness the powers of faith and gratitude. All of this will empower you to move toward your dreams with confidence and wisdom.

As you read, I hope you feel the creative fire that courses through me lighting your path. I have returned in voice and spirit to guide you. Now, let us begin the journey of Inspired Action together.
